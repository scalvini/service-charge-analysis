Questions for the managing agent

What does each management fee layer pay for that the others do not?

R&R charges £49,243 at estate level and £5,693 at building level. Why is there a further £4,289 on the affordable schedule? What does Peabody's £3,520 deliver on top?

Why are audit and H&S fees charged three times?

One auditor visits one building. One H&S consultant inspects one site. Why three fee lines? H&S alone costs Flat 17 £112 across the layers.

Why is there a separate arrangement fee for door inspections?

Coordinating contractors is core management work. R&R already receives £628 a year from Flat 17. A separate £16.19 charge is paying for the same task twice.

Why are fees rising faster than inflation?

Total management fees have risen 35% since 2022 against 18% CPI. With admin add-ons, the effective increase is 43%.
